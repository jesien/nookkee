Initiated as a hobby, Nookkee is the fixation of fleeting ideas and fantasies emerging as a result of a fortunate combination of place and mood.

The ideas of Nookkee refer to the basic needs of a settlement, when a travelling homosapiens chose a place based on his own needs. The era when survival and safety were top priorities gave place to the era of individualism, the dominance of self-expression and individual development.

The primary rule for Nookkee design is intravertism, the priority of individuality, and spiritual and moral tranquility.
